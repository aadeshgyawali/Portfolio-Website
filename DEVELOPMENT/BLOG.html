Internet Technology

Internet Technology is the hardware, software and network protocols that connect the computers around the globe. The Internet Technologies have been used in our lives for various purposes. Today's Internet Technologies like Electronic mail, Research, Download Upload, Online Maps, etc. have drastically changed our lives. The different Internet Technologies have made our lives easier and increased productivity. The research and development of The Internet Technologies has the power to change the world. But with the opportunities the Internet has provided there are good and bad aspect to it.

Advantages of Internet Technology

The positive side of the internet is the connection of billions of people. Internet has some key features like freedom of speech, automation, high speed data transfer, worldwide access, etc. Without the internet today’s world will be un-recognizable. The web provides billions of information from different people from around the globe. Newer technology like cloud computing gives us virtual storage. Accuracy plays a big part in today’s internet. When people require accurate data of something they search the internet. The front page of the internet is a hundred times more refined than local books. With the internet is always evolving we can search anything from out personal computer with an internet network. The internet also provides the source of Entertainment. Internet is one of the greatest invention of mankind and has a vital role in the advancement of our species.

Disadvantages of Internet Technology

The internet also has a dark side. Dark Web was first used by government to exchange information anonymously. Today, illegal crimes are are popular in the dark web. As many people of all ages and types use the internet, not all of them are aware of the dangers. Hacker’s gain information of the customer and visitor by doing a method known as Fishing. They are unaware that they have been hacked and may also result to identity thief. This result’s to the loss of bank information, email information, etc. The internet is also the home of cyberbullying. Internet is a place of free speech. People have different opinion of various topics. Spam and Advertisement has also degraded the quality of the Internet.
